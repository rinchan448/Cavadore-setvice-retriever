Why I Created the Cavadore Service Retriever

For years, I worked with service dogs and saw a gap — many people needed the intelligence, focus, and trainability of a Golden Retriever, but in a smaller, easier-to-handle size.

There are many wonderful small breeds, but it can be difficult to find one with the stable, dependable temperament of a Golden Retriever. Some toy-sized breeds are simply too tiny to safely navigate busy public environments, where they risk being stepped on or run over by carts.

The Cavadore Service Retriever bridges that gap — large enough to work confidently in public, yet small enough to live comfortably in any home or travel setting. By combining the gentle, people-focused temperament of the Golden Retriever, the soft expression and size of the Cocker Spaniel, and select traits from other small companion breeds, I created a dog under 30 pounds — without sacrificing brains, biddability, or work ethic.
